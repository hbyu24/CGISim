**Q1:** How do you plan to integrate more proactive exploration of alternative viewpoints and dissenting perspectives?

**Q2:** What steps will you take to improve responsiveness to market dynamics and capture more returns?

**Q2:** What specific steps will you take to ensure agility and responsiveness to changing market conditions?

*Dale Berry*

**A2:** I will propose a quarterly review of our investment efficiency versus market opportunities, and if our returns fall below a pre-defined threshold, we will mandate a strategic review and potential reallocation. This ensures our approach remains dynamic and data-driven.
