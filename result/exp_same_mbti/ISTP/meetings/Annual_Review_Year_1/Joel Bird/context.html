1. **Early Triggers & Sentiment Checks:**

We’ll track potential vs. actual returns after each quarter and debrief not just on what happened, but what would have happened under alternative choices. This will inform our future calibrations and help us close the gap toward maximum possible returns.

*Erin Fitzpatrick*
1. **Dissent & Scenario Stress-Testing:**
I’ll make dissent and scenario stress-testing a required part of our early deliberations. This means not just inviting, but expecting, shareholders to challenge prevailing assumptions and present alternative risk scenarios.
